About the Livingston Paranormal Society

The Livingston Paranormal Society was founded in 2008 by Michael Tracy, in the hopes of investigating paranormal activity in an unbiased, objective manner.

Our primary goal is to help, educate, and empower people. We do not charge for our investigations, as we feel that we should be there to help and educate those in our community and state. We only wish to find the truth, and assist those who need us however we can.

One of the main goals of the Livingston Paranormal Society is to prove or disprove the existence of paranormal activity by careful analysis of evidence, isolating and “debunking” all claims, until only the truth remains. Evidence remaining after this analysis may be considered paranormal, pending discovery of new evidence that indicates otherwise.

Evidence that is gathered during an investigation may include but is not limited to the list on the right. The goal is to produce consistent, repeatable results.

  • Witness testimony
  • Electronic Voice Phenomenon(EVP) and other audio sources
  • Unusual and unexplainable sources of electomagnetic fields (EMF)
  • Anomalous temperature readings without a source
  • Video and photographic evidence (including evidence from infrad, full spectrum, 3d, or thermal cameras)
  • Physical evidence that is measurable
