Welcome to the Livingston Paranormal Society!

Its been a while, but we are back! Weve been on a little hiatus due to covid, in order to keep everyone safe. Now that things have calmed down a bit, we will be looking for new members! This time it will be a litte different than before, however.

In the past, we took on members without any reflection on what jobs were needed. This resulted in jobs that didnt get done, and conflict over who would do them. Now that we are starting fresh, I think its better that we took on volunteers, specificly for the positions we need. This will not mean that they will be stuck in those positions, They will still be able to train for other jobs and eventually switch to their desired one in time. I believe this will make things work a little smoother. If you would like to volunteer for a position, Ill put up a page here with a private form for you to apply.

As far as this page goes, its brand new and under construction, As you can see with the logo, I'm trying out a few ideas to see how they look, This time I thought id try a "glitchy video" look. Hope you like it! Keep coming back to see new things, new buttons, and new pages :)
